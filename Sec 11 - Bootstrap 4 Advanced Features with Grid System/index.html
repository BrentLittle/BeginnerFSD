openpilot is open source software built to improve upon the existing driver assistance in most new cars on the road today. Tesla Autopilot like functionality for your Toyota, Honda, and more. openpilot is simple to use.

It enables your car to steer, accelerate, and brake automatically within its lane. Drive to a highway, press the cruise control SET button, and openpilot will engage. To disengage, use either pedal.
